CHAI-DATA PLATFORM AND STRATEGY

Clinton Health Access Initiative, Inc. collaborated with Statsspeak Group Limited, to enhance their data management capabilities. Statsspeak spearheaded several initiatives which included creation of a data centralization and integration strategy. They also constructed an on-premises data warehouse utilizing an open-source DBMS and developed an Extract, Transform, Load (ETL) pipeline to gather data from six systems such as DHIS2, eLMIS, and LIMs. Additionally, we designed a standardized Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) template for semi-automated cross-country program analyses and constructed machine learning algorithms, notably a forecasting and recommendation engine for Vaccines eLMIS. All the mentioned efforts resulted in enhanced data-driven insights and decision-making capabilities for Clinton Health Access Initiative, Inc.

AMREF-PCN DATA PLATFORM

Statsspeak partnered with Amref Health Africa, Kenya, to develop a cutting-edge dashboard for monitoring Primary Care Networks (PCNs) in alignment with Kenya's universal health coverage agenda and digital healthcare initiatives. This dashboard enables real-time tracking of critical healthcare elements like the referral system, maternal and child health outcomes and disease trends. By harnessing data from the dashboard, policymakers and healthcare providers gain valuable insights to make informed decisions and allocate resources strategically. The collaborative effort involved key stakeholders such as the Ministry of Health, county governments, and development partners, marking a significant milestone in advancing data-driven healthcare excellence and universal health coverage across Kenya.
