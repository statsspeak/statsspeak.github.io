DIGITAX
GO-TO MARKET STRATEGY

Statsspeak has partnered with DigiTax, an approved vendor by the Kenya Revenue Authority, to streamline tax compliance processes in Kenya. CHAI-DATA PLATFORM AND STRATEGY

Clinton Health Access Initiative, Inc. collaborated with Statsspeak Group Limited, to enhance their data management capabilities. Statsspeak spearheaded several initiatives which included creation of a data centralization and integration strategy.
They also constructed an on-premises data warehouse utilizing an open-source DBMS and developed an Extract, Transform, Load (ETL) pipeline to gather data from six systems such as DHIS2, eLMIS, and LIMs. Additionally, we designed a standardized Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) template for semi-automated cross-country program analyses and constructed machine learning algorithms, notably a forecasting and recommendation engine for Vaccines eLMIS. All the mentioned efforts resulted in enhanced data-driven insights and decision-making capabilities for Clinton Health Access Initiative, Inc .

AMREF-PCN DATA PLATFORM

Statsspeak partnered with Amref Health Africa, Kenya, to develop a cutting-edge dashboard for monitoring Primary Care Networks (PCNs) in alignment with Kenya's universal health coverage agenda and digital healthcare initiatives.
This dashboard enables real-time tracking of critical healthcare elements like the referral system, maternal and child health outcomes and disease trends. By harnessing data from the dashboard, policymakers and healthcare providers gain valuable insights to make informed decisions and allocate resources strategically. The collaborative effort involved key stakeholders such as the Ministry of Health, county governments, and development partners, marking a significant milestone in advancing data-driven healthcare excellence and universal health coverage across Kenya.

CREDIT SCORING ENGINE

Awash Bank, a leading financial institution in Ethiopia, aimed to modernize its services by introducing a mobile loan service. To ensure responsible lending, they needed a robust credit scoring system. They partnered with Statsspeak Kenya Limited to develop a hybrid system, combining traditional rule-based algorithms and advanced machine learning models. This approach allowed them to accurately assess the creditworthiness of potential borrowers. Through iterative testing and refinement, the system was optimized for accuracy. Moving forward, Statsspeak will continue to work with Awash Bank to adapt the system to changing customer behaviors and market conditions.
